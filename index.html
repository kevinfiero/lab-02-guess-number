Guess the Number!

Rules

  • Pressing the start button will generate a mysterious number between 1 and 20.
  • Your goal is to guess the number!
  • You will have 4 chances to guess correctly.
  • Use the text box to input your guess.
  • You will get feedback along the way on whether you should guess higher or lower.
  • After each round you will have the option to try again.
